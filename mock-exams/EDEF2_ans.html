$\displaystyle \sum_{r=1}^n \frac{2r+3}{3^r \cdot r(r+1)}=\sum_{r=1}^n \frac{1}{3^r}\left(\frac{3}{r}-\frac{1}{r+1}\right)=\sum_{r=1}^n \frac{1}{3^{r-1} \cdot r}-\frac{1}{3^r \cdot (r+1)}$ 1
$\displaystyle =\frac{1}{3^0 \cdot 1}-\frac{1}{3^1 \cdot 2}+\frac{1}{3^1 \cdot 2}-\frac{1}{3^2 \cdot 3}+\cdots+\frac{1}{3^{n-1} \cdot n}-\frac{1}{3^n \cdot (n+1)}$ 1
$\displaystyle =1-\frac{1}{3^n(n+1)}$ 1
